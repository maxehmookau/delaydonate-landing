How does this work?

If your train is more than 15 minutes late, we collect your compensation and donate it to charity. Simple.

How do I get involved?

  1. Tell us about your commute and give us your email address
  2. We'll keep an eye out to see if your trains to or from work are delayed
  3. We'll send you an email to check if you were on a delayed train
  4. We contact the train company on your behalf and fill in all the paperwork
  5. We collect the compensation owed to you and donate 100% of it to your chosen charity
